Toyota Concept-I Series
Toyota Concept-i and its forward-thinking UX hold a mirror up to a future that is warm,friendly,engaging abd,most of all-fun. Without further ado, meet Conceot-i.
The future has arrived.
While we can't predict the future, we can inspire it. So what wail our cars look like abd how will they function? Will we drive them or will they drive us? That seems to be the question on everyone's mind. Well, we have an idea. Cars are for people who want to go to more places safely and efficiently.
Less of a machine. More of a pal.
Concept-i follows our belief that vehicles shouldn't start with technology-they should start, and end, with the experience of the people who use them. Therefore, Concept-i was built from the inside out, with a focus an making it immersive, energetic and, most importantly-approachable.
